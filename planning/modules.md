# GrowthOS Modules

## 1. Dashboard

The central overview of each business workspace.

Displays:

- opportunity score
- market size
- target region
- target customer groups
- active risks
- recommendations
- uploaded documents
- active campaigns
- recent analysis

## 2. Business Workspace

Stores the core business idea and strategy.

Fields include:

- business name
- idea summary
- problem being solved
- proposed solution
- industry
- country
- region
- city
- target audience
- business model
- budget
- launch goal
- growth goal

## 3. Research

Collects and organizes market research.

Includes:

- market reports
- public datasets
- user-uploaded documents
- competitor information
- regulations
- funding opportunities
- demographic data
- tourism data
- economic indicators

## 4. Market Intelligence

Analyzes structured data to find:

- demand patterns
- underserved locations
- customer segments
- competitor density
- seasonal trends
- geographic opportunities
- risks
- anomalies

## 5. Knowledge

The existing PDF and document intelligence module.

Includes:

- upload
- extraction
- chunking
- embeddings
- semantic retrieval
- citations

## 6. AI Assistant

The existing grounded assistant.

Future responsibilities:

- business questions
- strategic recommendations
- research summaries
- risk explanations
- opportunity analysis

## 7. Marketing Studio

The existing marketing generation module.

Future capabilities:

- social posts
- email campaigns
- Google Ads
- landing-page copy
- campaign segmentation
- images
- short videos
- subtitles
- voice-over scripts

## 8. Projects

Tracks business execution.

Includes:

- goals
- tasks
- milestones
- owners
- deadlines
- progress

## 9. Customers

Stores customer segments and personas.

Includes:

- demographic profile
- location
- needs
- spending behavior
- travel behavior
- preferred channels
- campaign response

## 10. Finance

Future module for:

- startup budget
- expected revenue
- costs
- break-even estimates
- pricing scenarios
- financial forecasts
