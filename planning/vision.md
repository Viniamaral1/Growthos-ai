# GrowthOS Product Vision

GrowthOS is an AI Business Co-Founder that helps entrepreneurs and companies move from an idea to a validated, data-informed growth strategy.

The platform combines:

- business information
- uploaded company documents
- structured business data
- demographic and geographic data
- market research
- competitor intelligence
- AI-generated recommendations
- marketing content generation
- performance monitoring

## Core Promise

GrowthOS helps users understand:

1. What business opportunity exists.
2. Where the opportunity is strongest.
3. Which customer segments should be targeted.
4. What risks and competitors exist.
5. Which products, services, prices and campaigns should be used.
6. How the strategy should change as new data becomes available.

## Current Foundation

The current version already includes:

- company workspaces
- PDF ingestion
- text extraction
- semantic chunking
- embeddings
- document-scoped retrieval
- grounded answers with citations
- streaming local AI responses
- marketing content generation
- a professional Next.js interface

These features become the Knowledge and AI modules inside the larger GrowthOS platform.

## Long-Term Vision

GrowthOS will become a decision-intelligence platform that combines private company knowledge with structured and public market data to produce evidence-based business recommendations.

The platform should support:

- new business validation
- market selection
- geographic opportunity analysis
- customer segmentation
- competitor analysis
- pricing recommendations
- campaign creation
- AI-generated images and videos
- forecasting
- continuous performance improvement
